Plisse Details

Pleated blinds of the Amigo brand of this series are designed for installation on ceiling windows or glazed structures (winter gardens) with an angle of inclination of 45-90 °.
• These are tension-type models with fixing the curtain structure at the top and bottom (on a fixed cornice) Rectangular and complex shapes
• Reinforced tensioning system with steel cable ∅1 mm (white, gray and black) in silicone
• The cable tension can be adjusted using the adjusting screws. Raising and lowering the curtains is carried out by means of an electric drive built into the high upper eaves.
• The upper and lower position of the curtain is determined by the internal brake.
• Voltage: 12 V DC Remote control via wall switch/control panel Electric control can be organized as individual (for each curtain), group (for each group of curtains) or combined.
• Fixation of the fabric web is possible at any given level. The change in the height of the product is permissible within ± 5-10 mm (depending on the fabric) Measurements are made in the same way as conventional models
