Gofre Details

Amigo pleated blinds of this series are designed for installation on inclined windows with a tilt angle of up to 60°.
• These are tension-type models with fixation at the top and bottom (on the window sill bracket "shoe" or a fixed cornice) Rectangular models only.
• Closed cord tension system with springs, complete with a ∅1.2 mm side cord to prevent sagging of the mobile curtain rods.
• The curtain is controlled by a handle on a movable cornice / a pair of handles on 2 movable.
cornices: An additional handle is installed on the movable cornice with a curtain width of more than 80 cm Fixing the fabric web is possible at any given level. Change in product height is allowed within ± 5-10 mm (depending on the fabric) Day-night models using 2 fabrics, different in texture/color
