Horizontal

• Horizontal blinds are a well-known and popular type of modern sun protection product. The design of the blinds is made of steel profiles, built-in turning and lifting mechanisms built into the upper eaves, horizontally located slats connected to the canvas by means of strong ropes and a ladder. Turning and lifting the slats allows you to effectively adjust the light output, providing optimal sun protection and privacy. Horizontal blinds have high functional and decorative properties. They look great in modern interiors, emphasizing the purity and clarity of lines.

• The material of the lamellas can be aluminum tape, natural wood or bamboo, wood-like plastic. Thanks to their comfortable and easy operation, dimming functionality and ease of use, horizontal blinds are one of the most common products on the market today.
